Eliott Von Pine

Salut! I am Eliott, a master's student in economic research at IP Paris. My vision of a researcher's life is that it is alike that of an artist. While a researcher conducts experiments and publishes academic and scientific papers, the artist conducts projects and delivers visuals, music, and films. Both face this solitude, as both must immerse in a realm only them know, to produce singularity and genius. Although not of primary needs, art and research do contribute at making a life liveable and do shape society. In my works when modelling, designing, and writing, I try to create an art that can meet academic standards. As of now, I am still not sure that if I want to pursue a career in economic research, but if I do, I will try to breathe more art into science.
Research interests: Monetary Economics, Macroeconomics, Computational economics
